Tweet Builder

  • Enter your tweet text and your URL, both of which are required.
  • You can add a via name and a hashtag, if desired (no leading character is needed).
  • Your character count is recalculated as you move from field to field (you can use the [Tab] key to skip between them).
  • Once you have your fields set, click generate to build your tweet. The tweet will not generate if you're over the character count.
  • The tweets generated should be able to be copied and pasted into a spreadsheet.
